# v0.12.0 - Research Quality Upgrade

v0.12.0 adds deterministic research-quality fields and clearer valuation scenario context.

## Highlights

- Research CSV can now carry thesis, key risks, watch triggers, and follow-up questions.
- Memos include a Thesis Snapshot.
- Research packs include a Research Quality Overview.
- Valuation output includes fair-value range, margin-of-safety context, and valuation confidence.
- `docs/research-methodology.md` explains the tool's research process and boundaries.
