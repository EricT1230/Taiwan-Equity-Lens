# v0.4.0 - Research Workbench

v0.4.0 adds a local research workbench for tracking research states across a CSV stock universe.

## Highlights

- Research CSV template and validation.
- `research init`, `research run`, and `research summary` commands.
- Research summary JSON that connects research rows with workflow reliability status.
- Dashboard section for research state, priority counts, and attention items.
- Example research CSV for a deterministic local demo.

## Verify

```powershell
python -m unittest discover -s tests -v
```
