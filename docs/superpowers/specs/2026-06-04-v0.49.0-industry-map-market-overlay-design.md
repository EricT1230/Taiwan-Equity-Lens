# v0.49.0 Industry Map Market Overlay Design

v0.49.0 turns the existing Industry Map into a combined research map plus descriptive market-rotation overlay. The goal is not to rank sectors or recommend trades; it is to help the operator see which sectors are blocked for handoff, which experts raised blockers, and whether the sector has recent market context available for research review.

## Expert Review Lenses

- Product / UX: the first screen should keep answering "can this research be handed off, what is blocked, and what should I press next" while adding market context as an overlay, not a separate table.
- Finance research workflow: market returns and volume notes are descriptive inputs only. They must not become ratings, price targets, allocation guidance, or buy/sell/hold output.
- Engineering: v1 should be deterministic and local. Optional CSV fields are enough for immediate demo quality; richer market-data ingestion can be added later behind the same summary contract.

## Scope

- Add optional research CSV columns:
  - `market_return_1d`
  - `market_return_5d`
  - `market_return_20d`
  - `market_volume_signal`
  - `market_rotation_note`
- Parse percentage-like values into floats while preserving legacy CSV compatibility.
- Add per-stock `market_rotation` objects to research summaries.
- Add category-level `market_rotation_overlay` summaries:
  - coverage count,
  - missing-data count,
  - average 1D/5D/20D returns,
  - direction,
  - volume signals,
  - notes.
- Render the overlay inside Industry Map cards and detail panels.
- Add a market filter to the Industry Map.

## Non-Goals

- No live price feed or remote market-data dependency in v1.
- No sector ranking, recommendation engine, buy/sell/hold label, target price, or allocation guidance.
- No change to Handoff Quality Gate semantics.
- No new state store; research CSV and generated summary remain the source of the overlay.

## Stable Hooks

- `data-industry-map-filter="market"`
- `data-industry-map-market-direction`
- `data-industry-map-market-status`
- `data-industry-market-overlay="true"`
- `data-market-direction`
- `data-market-status`
